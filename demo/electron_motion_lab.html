THEORY NOTES

INDUCED E-FIELD

By Faraday's law & cylindrical symmetry:

Eᶜ = −½B₀ω r cos(ωt)

Vortex field, ∝ r inside cylinder.

EQUATION OF MOTION

Canonical angular momentum conserved:

φ̇ = ωc sin(ωt)

r̈ + ωc² sin²(ωt) r = 0

Mathieu equation → parametric oscillator.

CONFINEMENT

Stable when Mathieu parameter q avoids resonance bands:

q = ωc² / (4ω²) ∉ (0.46, 1.15)

ωc = eB₀ / (2m)

PERIODICITY

Radial & angular frequencies generally incommensurate:

ν — Floquet exponent (irrational)

Motion is quasi-periodic, trajectory never exactly closes.
